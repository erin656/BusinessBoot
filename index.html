Pelham Bay Business District

Since 2018, the Pelham Bay Business District (BID) has helped support restaurants, shops, and services along Westchester Avenue in the northeastern Bronx neighborhood of Pelham Bay. This growing commercial district combines the transportation access of Amendola Plaza, the natural beauty and amenities of Pelham Bay Park, and a small-neighborhood feel. The BID works with business owners and the community to develop the commercial corridor in ways that benefit the community and are consistent with the cherished history of the area.
